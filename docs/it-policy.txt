IT Support Policy & Knowledge Base

1. Password Management
- Reset Portal: https://password.example.com
- Policies: Passwords must be 12 characters, include specific characters. Expires every 90 days.
- Locked Out: Contact Helpdesk at 555-0199 or visit the IT Kiosk on the 2nd floor.

2. VPN Access (Remote Work)
- Client: Cisco AnyConnect Secure Mobility Client.
- Server Address: vpn.example.com (US), vpn-eu.example.com (Europe).
- Auth: Active Directory credentials + Duo 2FA push.
- Troubleshooting: If connection fails, check your internet connection first. Ensure you are not on a public network that blocks VPN ports.

3. Hardware Requests
- Standard Kit: MacBook Pro M3 (Devs), Dell XPS 15 (Business).
- Peripherals: One monitor, dock, and mouse provided. Headsets upon request.
- Broken Hardware: Report immediately via the portal. Do not attempt DIY repairs.
- Ticket Category: "Hardware > Damage/Repair".

4. Software & Access
- Approved List: Slack, Zoom, VS Code, Office 365, Adobe Creative Cloud.
- Request Process: Submit a "Software Access Request" in the portal. Manager approval required for paid licenses.
- Shadow IT: Unapproved software installation is strictly prohibited and monitored.

5. Wi-Fi Connectivity
- SSID: "CorpNet-Secure" (Employees), "Guest-Wifi" (Visitors).
- Login: Use your AD credentials for CorpNet.
- Issues: If signal is weak in a meeting room, please report the Room Number in your ticket.

6. Email & Communication
- Client: Outlook or Webmail (outlook.office.com).
- Phishing: Report suspicious emails using the "Report Phishing" button. NEVER share your password.
- Sync Issues: Restart Outlook. If persistent, rebuild your profile via Control Panel > Mail.

7. Escalation Matrix
- Tier 1: Chatbot / Knowledge Base (Immediate).
- Tier 2: Helpdesk Analysts (24-hour SLA).
- Tier 3: SysAdmins / Network Engineering (Critical Issues).
- Emergency: Call the NOC at 555-9111 only for system-wide outages.
